Bandmate's mission:

To connect people who are of like mind. To encourage collaboration. Like most things in life, we work better together. Music is no exception. Create, share, relate, and escape. Find your bandmate.

Search Locally

Bandmate is an app that works similar to dating apps, but rather than finding a local date, you are looking for local musicians.

Create a profile

Create a profile that highlights the instruments you play, what kind of music you are into, and what you are looking for in a bandmate.
